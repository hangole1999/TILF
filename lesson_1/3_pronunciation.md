## C Accent

> "C" pronounces "K" or "S" according to the consonants that follow it.

> To pronounce "C" as "S" requires "Cédille". (Leçon 1.4 Accent)

- \[ K \] Pronunciation
  - A
  - O
  - U
- \[ S \] Pronunciation
  - E
  - I
